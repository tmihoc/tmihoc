About

I am a linguist with an interest in free and open source software, and I am currently working as technical writer for the Juju team at Canonical.

For more details see below. For even more details, see my industry resume or my academic CV and visit me on Google Scholar, LinkedIn, or GitHub, or email me at te (dot) mihoc (at) gmail (dot) com.
