About

I am a linguist interested in formal, experimental, and computational modeling approaches to natural language semantics and pragmatics and in both fundamental and applied research.

Most of my past and ongoing work is about the pragmatic enrichment of logical meaning. Specifically, I show that this enrichment is often very similaracross different categories of language empirically, and I provide ways to unify it theoretically also.

For more details see my industry resume or my academic CV and visit my Google Scholar, LinkedIn, and/or GitHub profile.
